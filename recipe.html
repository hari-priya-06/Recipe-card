Chocolate Cake
Prep: 20 min Cook: 30 min Serves: 6
Ingredients
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
Steps
  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Mix dry ingredients together.
  • Beat in eggs, milk and melted butter.
  • Pour batter into greased pan.
  • Bake for 30 minutes.
  • Let cool, then serve.
